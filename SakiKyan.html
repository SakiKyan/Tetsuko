BACK　GROUND

Shuri High School

A prefectural high school in Okinawa. It can trace its roots to the Royal Academy of the Ryukyu Kingdom, which was established in 1798. Shuri High School's motto is "文武両道". "文武両道" is mean that It keep a balance between study and sports.

Okinawa International University

Okinawa International University is a private university in Okinawa,Japan. The predecessor of the school was founded in 1959, and it was chartered as a university in 1972. In keeping with the school's motto, “Striving for Autonomy and True Freedom”, Okinawa International University aims to become a community-centered institute open to the world.

AHGS English Academy

AHGS English Academy was established to provide Technical Vocational Education and Training programs specifically to train and tutor non-English speaking persons in English, engage in training of language teachers, conduct facilitation of interested students to study in Cebu, Philippines.
